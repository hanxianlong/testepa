Do Good – What’s That?

Warby Parker’s “do good” commitment applies to everything we do — from stimulating social entrepreneurship to sponsoring Little League teams (Go Royals!) and helping our employees to flourish.

Our Buy a Pair, Give a Pair program is one way we aim to create change on a global level. Warby Parker is also one of the only carbon-neutral eyewear brands in the world. We’re proud to be a certified B Corporation, which ensures that we meet the highest accountability standards for social and environmental practices.
